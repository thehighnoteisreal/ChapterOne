Silent No Longer

Posted: February 22

For the longest time, I’ve heard whispers from distant friends and other contacts that there is a force for good that has taken residence on the Internet. At first I couldn’t believe it, but then I began to see the evidence with my own eyes. There isn’t what the average person might see as “proof,” but the evidence is mounting, and for me it’s impossible to ignore.

My friends who believe the same thing as me have urged me to stay quiet. They claim that the world isn’t ready to know what we know. The personal risk is too much. But, I’ve seen so much that I need to spread the word.

And, that’s why I’ve started this blog.
